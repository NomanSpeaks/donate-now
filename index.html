Food for a family

Millions of families are teetering on the edge of food insecurity: Just 44% of households with children are “very confident” they can afford the food they need in the next four weeks, according to a census survey from November 2020.

Family Emergency Shelter

Families may access emergency shelter through the "Donate Now Family Resource Center". During the natural disasters, get support by calling the Shelter Hotline at (202) 399-7093. The shelter hotline is open 24 hours a day.

Invested in education

Our tutoring programs provide children the assistance and attention they need to graduate from secondary school and beyond. It helps them create a path out of poverty for themselves and their families.
